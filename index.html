About the Chamber

A Legacy of Legal Excellence in Bihar

The Chamber of Adv. Rajeev Kumar Dwivedi — widely known across Bihar as Pappu Dubey — is a distinguished legal practice headquartered in Motihari. Led by Adv. Rajeev Kumar Dwivedi and a team of dedicated associates, the Chamber has built a reputation for rigorous legal advocacy, principled counsel, and steadfast commitment to every client's cause.

With over 20 years of courtroom experience, Adv. Rajeev Kumar Dwivedi (Pappu Dubey) has handled thousands of matters spanning criminal defence, family law, personal injury, and a wide range of civil and miscellaneous cases. His name is trusted by individuals, families, and institutions across East Champaran and beyond.

Adv. Rajeev Kumar Dwivedi @ Pappu Dubey currently serves as the General Secretary of the Motihari District Bar Association and is an Ex-Member of the Bihar State Bar Council — a distinction that sets him apart as the only advocate from Motihari ever to have been elected as a member of the Bihar State Bar Council. This rare honour is a testament to the trust, respect, and standing he commands across the legal community of Bihar.

We believe that access to quality legal representation is a right, not a privilege — and we deliver it with integrity and diligence at every step.
